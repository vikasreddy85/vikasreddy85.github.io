I am a first-year Master's student in the Department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University. I am currently based in Baltimore, Maryland but also frequently visit my home in Tokyo, Japan. I speak four languages, including French and Japanese, and love traveling whenever I get the chance.

My research interests focus on Machine Learning, Data Science, and Computational Social Science. I completed my undergraduate degree at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I worked with Prof. Giovanni Luca Ciampaglia to study news reliability on Bluesky Social Media.
